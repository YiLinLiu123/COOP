Propeller blade (front view)
Propeller curves and cross sections

This propeller is constructed using MATLAB generated curves as well as two airfoil profiles. A simple loft is done between the two profiles using the two curves as paths.

2D ellpitical propeller curves
NACA-2411 airfoil
resultant transformations

Two elliptice offsetted from the origin are used as propeller curves as recommended by proffessors. The NACA airfoil ordinates are obtained online. A series of linear transformations are applied to both the arifoil as well as the ellpitical curves to obtain the correct twist angle and skew at each cross section.
